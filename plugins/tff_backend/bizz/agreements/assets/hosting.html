WHEREAS

The Company is building its first blockchain running on a grid of connected devices called ThreeFold Nodes. In order to achieve a geographical spread of this grid, the Company wishes to host the ThreeFold Nodes with various hosters.

Host is willing to host one ThreeFold Node in exchange for a certain amount of ThreeFold Tokens, all as set out hereinafter.

NOW THEREFORE in consideration of the premises, mutual covenants and agreements herein and other good and valuable consideration, the sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

1. Definitions

- ‘Software’ means the software running on the Threefold Node, consisting of the Company’s Zero-OS, blockchain and related software.

- ‘ThreeFold Node’ means the hardware provided by the Company to the Hoster in view of its hosting pursuant to this Agreement.

- ‘ThreeFold Token’ means smart contract digital token developed and issued by the ThreeFold Foundation as a digital currency (the Token).

2. Delivery and Services

The Company shall ship one ThreeFold Node to the Host. Company is and remains the owner of the ThreeFold Node.

Host shall provide the following services to the Company:

  • physically hosting the Threefold Node in a secure indoor environment;
  • keeping the Threefold Node plugged into an electrical grid at the required service voltage;
  • keeping the Threefold Node connected to the required network infrastructure (i.e. broadband internet connectivity);

3. Consideration

In consideration for the Services provided, the Company agrees to grant six (6) ThreeFold Tokens per year to Host. The ThreeFold Tokens will be granted at the end of each relevant year on the anniversary date of this Agreement, provided the Host has duly completed its obligations set out under section 2 above.

4. Term of this Agreement

This Agreement is entered into for 5 years, unless terminated by either party in accordance with section 9 herein (the “Term”).

5. Limitation

The Host undertakes not to host more than one ThreeFold Node per address and/or physical location, and not to connect more than one ThreeFold Node to each individual internet connection.

6. Third Party Software Prohibited

The Host is strictly prohibited from installing any third party software on the ThreeFold Node without the express written authorization of the Company.

10. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at anytime on thirty (30) days written notice to the other. In addition thereto, the Company can immediately terminate this Agreement and withdraw the ThreeFold Node in the event of a material breach of the Hoster of any provisions of this Agreement, or when the Company, in the sole discretion, determines that: (i) the Host is using or allowing, authorizing or assisting the ThreeFold Node to be used for illegal purposes; or (ii) the Host downloads or installs third party software to the ThreeFold Node without the express written authorization of the Company.

Upon termination of this Agreement the Host will immediately return the ThreeFold Node to the Company.
